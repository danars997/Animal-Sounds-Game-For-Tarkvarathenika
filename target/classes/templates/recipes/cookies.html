INGREDIENTS

granulated sugar, brown sugar, butter, vanilla, egg, flour, baking soda, salt, coarsely chopped nuts, chocolate chips

RECIPE

1.Heat oven to 375єF.
2.Mix sugars, butter, vanilla and egg in large bowl. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt (dough will be stiff). Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
3.Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet.
4.Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown (centers will be soft). Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet. Cool on wire rack.
